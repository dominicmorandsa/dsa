Favourite Dinner

Beef Stew


Preparation time 15 minutes, cooking time 2 hours 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 25 g plain flour
  • 1 kg stewing beef
  • 75 ml olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 700 ml stock
  • salt and pepper
  • 700 g potatoes
  • 500 g carrots

Preparation

  1. Cut beef into chunks and coat with flour
  2. Skin and chop onion
  3. Skin and finely chop garlic
  4. Peel potatoes and cut into chunks
  5. Peel carrots and cut into chunks

Cooking

  1. Heat oil in casserole
  2. Add chunks of beef a few at a time, removing as they brown
  3. Add onion and garlic, cooking until tender
  4. Stir in stock, salt and pepper, cook until slightly thickened
  5. Return meat to casserole, bring to boil, simmer for 2 hours
  6. Add potatoes and carrots, stir well and bring back to the boil
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes until beef and vegetables are tender
